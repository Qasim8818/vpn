THE ARCHITECTURE
================
Client sends packet → VPN tunnel → (optionally P4 NIC) → WireGuard daemon
  (with Kyber PSK, Blind token auth, QUIC transport, RPKI route auth)
  with seccomp containment, eBPF observability, MPC distributed keys

Against threat models:
  • ISP: QUIC on port 443 defeats DPI
  • Firewall: obfuscation + domain fronting
  • Local attacker: multipath failover + correlation protection
  • Global adversary: needs mixnet for timing resistance
  • Server compromise: MPC keys can't be stolen, seccomp limits RCE
  • Quantum threat: Kyber PSK protects current traffic immediately
